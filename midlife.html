Heart Attack Symptoms Women may Not Recognize

Learn how to recognize the early and subtle warning signs of heart attacks in women. You could help prevent a tragedy by picking up on these surprising signals. Every year, about 435,000 women in the US have heart attacks, which kill 6 times more women than breast cancer. Of these women, 71% experience early warning signs like extreme fatigue, but many don't recognize their symptoms as signs of a heart attack.
When you imagine a heart attack, you might picture what you see in the movies: a man clutching his chest in pain, followed by the discovery of a big clot in an artery on a EKG.
